Several interactive projects using p5.js, Processing & Unity.

Music Under New York

Music Under New York is a data visualization project that aims to use different platforms to showcase the New York subway busking music. The website acts like an online music player. The music data recorded by myself. The location data is from NYC Open Data.

Tool: p5.js, mapbox.js

Click, Yellow!

Click, Yellow! is a website that was originally designed to gain more intentions in the color-blind community through games.There are a lot of people in the world who have a hard time recognizing colors and can't find the yellow block as easily as we can.

Underwater Peace

Underwater Peace is a concept game and an experiment for me to explore various forms of interactive games.The concept is to construct a peaceful game free of complicated controls or intense story lines, in order for players to take a break from their fast-paced lives. At the same time I hope to remind people to protect our ocean ecosystem.

Tool: Unity
