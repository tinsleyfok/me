COCO & Her Scissor

After Reading Coco Chanel's biography, I made my own booklet about her and her scissors. She used her scissor to reshape her creations and free women from their clothes; She cut her hair and her tie to family and miserable childhood to free herself. In the process of designing this book, I focus on the typographic Hierarchy, working with its scale and composition.
