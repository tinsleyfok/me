Summer & Fall 2019, I interned at the Tech team in Figure8.

Figure8 is a experience first development company that builds spaces for people. Museum of Ice Cream opened its doors in the summer 2016, is the first project by Figure8. MOIC right now has two premenent locations in SF & NYC and plan to expand globally. My first project in this company was to redesign MOIC's website.

Also, I worked on a enterprise application for the company internal use. This interface offers connected control functions to optimise visitor experience, control and maintain the building and rides, as well as centralize staffs and operations management.

ROLE: UI/UX Design & Prototyping

The project is ongoing. I can only show the information architecture and my design process at this moment. However, I would more than willing to talk more details privately.

Information Architecture

This information architecture is based on my understanding of the project as well as the guidelines our strategist provided. MOID is the temporary name of the tech integration for the whole museum and I am responsible for the UX of the Control Center Dashboard.

Design Process
