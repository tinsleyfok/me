Fingerprint is an interactive website that can generate a unique fingerprint pattern according to the user’s favorite color and birthday. The purpose of the website is to show everyone’s uniqueness, just like their fingerprints.

While at Bootcamp, I found that everyone here are so different. We had different backgrounds and strong points. We are in the same program but are all very unique. This was what inspired me to build this website.

The creative coding is inspired by Yasai’s open source Perlin Noise. I used libraries P5.js , TweenMax.js and Cropper.js to build the website.

Generate your unique fingerprint!
