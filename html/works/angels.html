The Angels



DESCRIPTION

The Angel is an experimental installation built at the bus stop. By using force sensor with Arduno board, the angel wings behind the bench will light up when someone sits down.

Everyone are angels, even though we tend to forget. We are always angels to the ones closest to us - our children, our parents, or our significant others. But we can be even more. We can be angels to strangers around us. I wish to remind people to share the love with not just their closest ones, but also with those that they may very well never meet again.
