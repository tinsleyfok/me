Shanghai Skyline


The Shopping Bag that Detect Air Pollution



DESCRIPTION

The original idea was an interactive website that uses minimalistic graphics to visualize the skyscrapers of Shanghai. The idea of the shopping bag that responds to changes in air quality came to me when I noticed the Air Quality Index(AQI) in Shanghai is at its worst state to date. There are many other cities in the world plagued by pollution as a consequence of developing without concerns for the environment, and I wish to remind people of their individual duties on preserving our eco-system.
