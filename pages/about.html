Hello, My NameIs Tinsley.

STATEMENT

Tinsley is a multidisciplinary interaction designer with an emphasis on user experience design and interface design for digital screen device and others new technology such as augmented reality, machine learning and virtual reality. She is obsessed with visually creative coding, in love with data visualization, and enthusiastic about music.

Her potential for creating is endless. And she believes the potential of combining design with technology is never-ending.



EXPERIENCE

Tinsley currently is a MFADT student at Parsons School of Design. She was graduated from UC Davis, with a double major in design and communication. She worked as a visual designer for a year in Los Angeles after graduating from college.
