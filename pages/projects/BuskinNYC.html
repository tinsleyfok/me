BuskinNYC

YEAR: 2019(Ongoing) | ROLE: UI/UX Design | TOOL: Sketch




INTRODUCTION

BuskinNYC is an App-based community which connects subway performers and their audiences. The communicty aims to help performers who participate in MTA music program(Music Under New York) gain more audience and widely spread these joyous and engaging subway music.

BACKGROUND

Since 1985, Arts & Design has managed the Music Under New York (MTA MUSIC) program, to bring joyous and engaging music to the commuting public. Currently, more than 350 soloists and groups participate in MTA MUSIC providing over 7,500 annual performances at 30 locations throughout the transit system. It's a large group of talented performers, but they do not have a community to showcase their talents. Even though their performance is not the best, they bring lots of joy to people who pass by the subway station.
