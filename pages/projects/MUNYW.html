Music Under New York - Website

2018 | Javascript



DESCRIPTION

Music Under New York is data visualization project that aims to use different platform to showcase the New York subway busking music.

The website version, which spread easily and widely, condenses the underground music world of New York to a website. In the future, I hope it can also become a platform for people to upload busking music themselves.
