Transo
Your futuristic travel app.

BRIEF

Transo is a speculative time-travel booking system. It is one-week design challenges for Figure-8 UX Design intern candidates in 2019. App Transo later won me a chance to intern at Figure-8.

ROLE

UX Design
Prototyping

PROMPT

A time-traveling booking service interface for people wanting to experience traveling through time. This could be a first time adventure, or set in a time when time-travel is normalized. Consider what time travel will be like: where will users want to travel? What is the price point of a trip? How long are trips? Are there limitations for time travel? How safe is time travel? What are the rules and regulations of time travel?



INTERPRETATION & CONSTRAINTS

  • Time Period: Future when time-travel is normalized.
  • Whom: People who want to explore time-travel.

    • RESEARCH

    I started by interviewing people age range from 18 to 40 that potential have interests and also can afford future time-travel. Based on the interview and research, I concluded three groups of potential user for the time-travel booking service.

    IDEATION

    Playfulness and gamification can be employed not only in video games, but also in other kinds of products to create a delightful and memorable experiences for users. Features that make games engaging can also make other kinds of products more enjoyable, meaningful and memorable, strengthen users’ future positive actions and ultimately increase the quality of overall user experience and the market value of a product.

    Based on The Playful Experience(PLEX) framework, I add playful features that usually applied to games to a speculative booking system, in order to design joyous and remarkable interface and user experience.

    MAIN FEATURES

    Based on the persons, I designed three features:




    • Customized (PLEX framework: control)
    • Discover (PLEX framework: discovery)
    • Random (PLEX framework: Thrill)

    Cutomized
    • PLEX framework: control - Dominating, commanding,regulating
    • Target Users: know exactly where to go
    • User Flow: choose location(map) → choose dates → select duration(see the price) → pay → get ticket






    Discover
    • PLEX framework: discovery - Finding something new or unknown
    • Target Users: users not sure where to go
    • User Flow: Spin the wheel to browse the options → see the detailed description(time period/location/price/duration → book→ pay and confirm → get ticket






    Random
    • PLEX framework: Trill- Excitement derived from risk and danger
    • Target Users: user don’t know where to go / don’t want to go
    • User Flow: Spin the wheel → know the basic description of the trip(future or past/continent/price/duration → book→ pay and confirm → get ticket(know exactly where to go)






    Character
    • PLEX framework: humor & fellowship
    • Natural language process
    • Function:Tell you the rules, help you sign in/sign up, tell your the tips about the trips etc during the booking system.
