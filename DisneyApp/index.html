My Disney Experience

BRIEF

My Disney Experience is the App for Disneyland in Orlando. In late 2019, with the help of two UX designers, I performed detailed usability assessments of the current App and redesigned its UX from the ground up. This case study covers the redesign process and focuses on the feature called “My Plan”.

ROLE

UX Design
Prototyping

OVERVIEW

The App has five main features: Home, Map, Shortcut, Search & User Account. For pre-experience, users are able to use the App to buy tickets, get fastpass+, check the attractions information, shop and plan my the trip. During the experiences, users can get direction from the map and order food beforehand, while for the post-experience, users can easily check their photos via the App. The App seems to be prefect.




CURRENT DESIGN

"My Plan" is currently under User Account section and has lots of limitations:

For those who do not buy tickets: Almost all the attractions cannot be added/saved to the "My Plan". For those who have already bought the tickets, This function is only for you to get the fastpass+, reserve dinner and link the Disney hotel that you already booked.

In other words, this is not a function to plan a trip in Disney.

REDESIGN

We started by redesigning the structure of the App. All the users including those who don't buy tickets but plan to go to Disneyland can plan their trip via the App. For users who love to plan in advance, the App will send the notification once the fastpass+ of rides in the "My Plan" portal is available. For users who don't like planning, they still can get fastpass+ directly in the fastpass+ portal. In this way, users who love planning can easily plan their trip and get fastpass+ beforehand.





WIREFRAME

Add Plan Flow:
1. Easily add the attractionz to the "My Plan" Portal from home page.
2. Go to "My Plan" from shortcut.
3. Search attraction insides "My Plan".




View & Edit Plan Flow:
1. View the schedule by date
2. Easily change the plan by drag, hold and move.




Get & Change Fastpass+:
1. Alert when the fastpass is available
2. Easily cancel and change the time of the fastpass+
